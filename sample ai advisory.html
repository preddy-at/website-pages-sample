Frequently Asked Questions

How do you ensure our data won’t be used to train third-party models?
We establish hard data boundaries and route traffic through governance-controlled gateways with no-retain policies. No customer data is used to train external models without your explicit approval.
How do you prevent vendor lock-in?
We are vendor-neutral and design with portability in mind, using abstraction layers and open standards that allow you to switch models or platforms as needed.
How do you prove ROI and avoid “AI theater”?
Each use case starts with a baseline, a target, and a measurement plan. We track benefit realization (cost savings, revenue, productivity) and will pivot if the data doesn’t justify scaling.
