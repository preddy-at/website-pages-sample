Frequently Asked Questions

How is Heimdall deployed?

Heimdall can be deployed as an inline reverse-proxy gateway, a sidecar per service, or an SDK. It is cloud-agnostic (AWS/Azure/GCP) and supports on-premise, air-gapped, Kubernetes, and serverless environments.

What models and frameworks are supported?

We support major providers like OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, and open-source models via vLLM/TGI. We also support orchestration frameworks like LangChain and LlamaIndex.

What is the performance impact?

The inline firewall typically adds a p50 latency of ~3-8 ms per request path. The system is designed for horizontal autoscaling to handle high throughput with minimal overhead.

How do you prevent data leakage?

We use a multi-layered approach including DLP detectors for PII/secrets, context scoping with role-based access control (RBAC), and output filters that enforce confidentiality policies.
